Victoria Square tops out

The Victoria Square mixed use development in Woking, UK, has reached a major milestone this week with the formal topping out ceremony. Benoy Director Robert Bentley and Associate Director Vickie Cheung attended the ceremony. 

Once complete, Victoria Square will consist of three towers providing contemporary residential, retail, and hotel units, making up a key part of the Woking Town Centre Masterplan.

Find out more about the project here, and follow @WeAre_Woking to find out about some of the latest developments in the Woking community.
