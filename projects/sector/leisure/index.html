Leisure

As boundaries blur within this leisure and F&B, Benoy is adept at creating visionary places and spaces where people want to be. We have the fluidity of approach required to exploit the cross-sector synergies and overlaps inherent within today’s urban leisure environment. Our work in residential, retail and workspace enable us to develop multidimensional designs that are responsive to the diverse needs of the end user.
